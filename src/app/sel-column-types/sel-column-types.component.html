Information Regarding Data Scales
Nominal Data

Nominal data is non numerical data, e.g. the name of a district, the type of rooms etc.

Ratio Data

Ratio data are numerical, and "zero" means "non-existence", e.g., 0 population means "no people", 0 dollar means "no money".

Interval Data

Interval data are numerical, and "zero" does not mean "non-existence", e.g. in case of the IQ test of a person, the 0 does not mean "no intelligence at all", similarly 0 degree celsius temperature does not mean "non-existence of temperature".
